Loaded Breakfast Burritos

Recipe for 8 loaded burritos!

Ingredients

  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 500g Lean beef mince
  • 800g chopped potatoes
  • .5 Chopped Onion
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Paprika
  • 1 Tsp Mixed Herbs
  • 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 8 Eggs
  • 100g Cream Cheese
  • 80g tomato paste

Steps

  1. Dice and chop potatoes and onion
  2. Season Potatoes and Beef with spice
  3. Cook beef with onions
  4. Airfry potatoes 24 min 200 degrees C, flip half way
  5. Scramble eggs with cheese on low heat, make puffy
  6. Distribute in 8 wraps
  7. head tortilla on high heat to seal burrito
  8. Wrap in tinfoil and freeze
