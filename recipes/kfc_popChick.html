Popcorn Chicken Bowl Pre-Meal

Pre-meal preparation for the KFC Popcorn Chicken Bowl.

Description

This easy recipe will serve make 4 servings, easy to conserve in the fridge and warm up when needed

  • 800g Diced Chicken Breast
  • 150g Flour
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 Egg
  • 50g Light Mayo
  • 20g Sriracha
  • 1.6kg Diced Potatoes

Instructions

  1. Season the Flour with the spices above
  2. Coat the chicken in the the egg
  3. Coat the chicken in the flour mixture
  4. Air Fry 23 minutes, turn half half way at 200 degrees
  5. Season diced potatoes with above seasoning
  6. Airfry for 25 minutes, move around half way
  7. Combine the light mayo and sriracha in a bowl
  8. Combine the chicken and potatoes in a bowl
  9. Drizzle the sauce over the top
  10. Enjoy!
