Philly Cheese Steak Pasta

Prep Meal delicious steak pasta, this recipe makes 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 600g Steak
  • .5 Onion
  • 250g Mixed peppers
  • 150g cream cheese
  • 350g pasta
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Black pepper
  • 1 Tsp Garlic powder
  • 1 Tsp Italian seasoning
  • 50g Mozarella Cheese Shredded

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente. Drain
  2. Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add steak and season with spices, cook until browned.
  3. Add onion and peppers to the pan and cook until softened.
  4. Add Chreaam and Mozarella cheeses
  5. Add some pasta water and mix till creamy
  6. Combine cooked pasta and steak mixturet
  7. Separate in 4 equal servings, allow to cool and store in fridge
